California Roll Recipe

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 8 sticks imitation crab
  • 2 tbsp kewpie mayo
  • 1 julienned cucumber
  • 1 avocado
  • Nori sheets
  • 4 tbsp sesame seeds
  • soy sauce (side)

    Steps

  1. Cook and prepare your sushi rice.
  2. Cut up imitation crab sticks into small pieces and put them in a small bowl.
  3. Add your kewpie mayo into the bowl and mix to combine.
  4. Cut up your avocado intoo thin slices and julienne your cucumber into thin slices.
  5. Cur the nori sheets into half, hamburger style.
  6. Take 1/2 cup of rice and using your hands, layer the rice evenly onto the nori, placed over a sushi mat.
  7. Sprinks sesame seeds over the rice.
  8. Flip the nori and rice over and add the crab mixture along the middle of the nori.
  9. Layer the avocado and cucumber slices horizontally along the crab mixture.
  10. Roll your sushi using the mat as a guide.
  11. Cut up your sushi rolls.
  12. Plate your rolls and enjoy with a side of soy sauce.
