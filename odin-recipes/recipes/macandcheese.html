Simple Mac and Cheese Recipe

This super simple mac and cheese recipe makes for a quick and delicious lunch.

Ingredients

  • 450g macaroni
  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 680g grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add salt and oil to a pot of water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add macaroni and cooked for directed time until al dente.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt yout butter, add flour and whisk over medium high heat to form a paste.
  4. Stir until sauce thickens and add cheese in increments.
  5. Add garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  6. Mix cheese sauce int pasta and mix until evenly coated.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
